What is Digitool

Digitool is a Social Enterprise facilitating collaboration between clients (business owners), consultants (fledgling entrepreneurs) and training.

CONSULTANT

We create positive employment for young and/or unemployed people who are looking for an opportunity to work in digital marketing. They are our “Consultants”.

CLIENT

Our “Clients” are businesses who need help with their online presence. They need a new website and/or better social media presence at an affordable price point and in a relatively short timeframe.

Affordable cost

Clients are offered services such as Website Design and Development and Social Media Management at relatively low prices, and consultants can get paid while learning jobs.

GET ONLINE

Consultants gain employment, learn, grow and build confidence. Clients improved online presence and more visibility and customers.

Our Client

Digitool is changing the way business becomes digital, and students become employable. It specialise in helping businesses grow. They do that by linking businesses with a team of students who get the techie stuff (with ease). They support the business owner to develop a strategy and we guide the students to implement the strategy.

Project Aims

The main aims of this project will be to update and centralise the core framework of digitool, while updating the Graphical User Interface(GUI) to bring a better user experience and extending the program with additional functions. The core features to be added include:



  • A reworked messaging system with drag and drop file attachment,
  • An improved file storage system,
  • A rework/refine of the Access Control System(ACS),
  • The implementation of project templates, project modification and a searchability function.
